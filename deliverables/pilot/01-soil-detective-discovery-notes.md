## Glossary (read once, then skim)

- **Patreon** — a subscription platform creators use to charge fans for ongoing access to content.
- **Brix testing** — a method for measuring sugar content in plant juice, used in regenerative agriculture as a soil-health indicator.
- **Adobe Fonts** — Adobe's web font library; "Flower Power" is a specific font from that library.
- **Membership tier** — the standard subscription-product structure where higher prices unlock more access.
- **The Soil Builders Guild** — Rosalinda's business name; doing business as Soil Detective for the consumer-facing brand.
- **TB** (terabyte) — 1,000 gigabytes. 2 TB of video is roughly 600–2,000 hours depending on resolution.
- **Q&A bot** — a chatbot trained on a body of content that answers questions in the original author's voice.

## Pain points captured (verbatim, not yet analyzed)

### 1. Patreon dormancy

- Started Patreon in 2023.
- **Has not touched it since 2023.**
- Wants automated content publishing into Patreon — currently a complete content channel sitting unused.

### 2. 2 TB of video sitting unused

She has roughly **2 terabytes of raw video** that needs to be repurposed into three output streams:

- **Community content** — short-form for the membership audience.
- **Advertising** — outbound marketing assets.
- **Educational curriculum** — long-form structured learning material.

### 3. Memberships

- **Likes the rebuild's restructure** of "membership → community."
- **HATES taking money.** This is a quote-worthy structural pain. A workable membership model has to make charging feel comfortable to her.

**Proposed tier structure** (from the handwritten draft she provided):

| Tier | Annual fee | Monthly equivalent | For |
|---|---|---|---|
| Seed | $60 | $5 | Beginners exploring soil health and gardening |
| Sprout | $180 | $15 | Hands-on participants |
| Root | $360 | $30 | Serious gardeners and food growers |
| Mycelium | $720 | $60 | (TBD — appears in handwritten draft but not yet typed-up benefits) |
| Forest | $1,200 | $100 | (TBD — appears in handwritten draft but not yet typed-up benefits) |

**Typed-up benefits document (her draft):**

- **Seed ($60/yr):** community newsletter, seasonal educational resources, member pricing on workshops, invitations to community gatherings.
- **Sprout ($180/yr):** everything in Seed + participation in hands-on compost building workshops + hands-on demonstrations + garden and compost skill-building activities.
- **Root ($360/yr):** everything in Sprout + advanced workshops + soil biology and microscopy experiences + community project participation + priority registration for special events.
- **Mycelium / Forest:** to be defined.

**Handwritten draft mentions:**
